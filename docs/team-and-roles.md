# Bridge Team — Team & Roles

## Why this situation exists

SPS acquired Carbon6 (C6) — Radmilo and his Serbia-based team (Nikoleta, Predrag, Lazar) continued from C6 as contractors to SPS. SPS also acquired SupplyPike — Katie became a full-time SPS employee through that acquisition. Web sits inside the COE (Centre of Excellence) team. The head of COE placed Joe, Katie, and Radmilo on top of web together.

The web function at SPS has been broken for approximately 6 months. Before this team, Joe was the only web-side employee and operated by working with agencies. SPS had no real focus on digital marketing or web. Radmilo's initiative — the bridge project — is a deliberate move to take lead and fix the broken system before it gets worse.
